The Nature of Autumn

Jean Traudt

My style is called The Nature of Autumn and is based on the Fall season-inspired Charley Harper Art pieces.

I drew inspiration from Charley Harper's autumn art pieces of nature such as for Cincinnati Nature Center and Isle Royale National Park.

In the map, yellow leaves represent major cities.

The map encapsulates some of the animals such as Moose as you zoom in.

Take a look at a few different geographies, starting with Madison, Wisconsin. This area showcases the form as smooth, simple, and clear lines.

Part of the United Kingdom. The roads are a yellow-orange to replicate the fall-colored leaves.

The greens from the frog and background are seen at low zoom levels such as in Greenland. This area also highlights the sans serif, evenly spaced lettering font that is easy to read. The typefaces I use are Made Tommy and Monsterrat.
